1. Introduction

Welcome to Coach L Academy (coachl-academy.github.io). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

By accessing our courses, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

Coach L Academy is a free educational platform. We do not require account registration or collect personal data to access course content.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide

Some pages may ask for a name or nickname to personalise your experience. This information is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

2b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)

This data is collected anonymously via Google AdSense and is used for advertising purposes only.

2c. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your course progress (completed lessons, assessment scores). This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this platform. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visits to our site
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings
  • You may also opt out via aboutads.info
Our ads.txt file is publicly available at coachl-academy.github.io/ads.txt and confirms that Google (pub-1566975054213856) is an authorised seller of our advertising inventory.

4. Cookies

We do not set first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for advertising personalisation. You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Settings → Safari → Privacy

Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use course content.

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform may link to or embed content from the following third-party services:

  • YouTube — for embedded lesson videos (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • GitHub Pages — for hosting this platform

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Some of our courses are designed for teen and young adult audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal security risk to your personal information. All course progress data is stored locally on your device and is under your control.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local course progress data at any time via browser settings
  • Opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ad Settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
